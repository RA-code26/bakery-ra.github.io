Featured Recipe: Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients

  • 100g softened butter
  • 85g light brown sugar
  • 40g caster sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 125g self-raising flour
  • 100g plain flour
  • baking powder
  • pinch of salt
  • chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Mix the butter, light brown sugar and caster sugar
  3. Add the egg
  4. Mix the dry ingredients until combined
  5. Add the chocolate chips
  6. Fold in

Featured Recipe: Brownies

Ingredients

  • 135g melted butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2tsp vanilla
  • 120g melted chocolate
  • 3/4 cup plain flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • Choclate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Mix the butter and sugar until combined
  3. Add the eggs and the vanilla and mix until combined
  4. Add in your dry ingredients
  5. Add the chocolate chips
  6. Fold in
