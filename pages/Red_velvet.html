Featured Recipe: Red Velvet cake

Ingredients

  • 2½ cups (315 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1½ cups (300 g) granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder
  • 1½ cups (360 ml) vegetable oil
  • 1 cup (240 ml) buttermilk, room temperature
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 2 tbsp red food coloring (liquid)
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Mix the dry ingredients and leave aside
  3. Mix the wet ingredients together
  4. Add both the dry and wet ingredients together
